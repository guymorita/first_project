Oh hi herro prease

Welcome to the hi herro page. Here we will go into detail about what goes into making a great juice. Juice is basically a way to eat (drink) healthy. It allows you to extract the nutrients from several pounds of nutrients into a quick, on-the-go drink.

I have juice every morning of everyday. I think it's one of the best ways I've found to feel energetic throughout the day and not to crash at 5pm.

Here is my recipe for my morning juice:

  • One Beet
  • One Cucumber
  • Five Stalks of Celery
  • One Lemon
  • One Chunk of Ginger (one square inch)
  • Three Carrots
